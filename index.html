Welcome

With the closure of all schools, we know that this will be a challenging time for many parents and carers. Our theatre is currently closed for public performances, but our staff are super creative people and they will be using their time to generate a whole host of stay-at-home activity guides to help keep you and your little ones entertained.

Shows

Little Angel Theatre has partnered with theatre director Ian Nicholson to stream an adaptation of Jon Klassen’s picture book I Want My Hat Back.
The performance will be availbale from Easter Monday, 13 April at 11am BST and will be available until June 2nd.
It will be free to access and is aimed at children aged 3-6. Nicholson will be directing and performing the show from home during lockdown, with set designed by Samuel Wilde and music by Jim Whitcher.

Stories

Over the coming weeks we will be releasing a daily video story told to camera by one of our talented performers.

Projects

Here we can find longer term projects spend an hour each day with children to make a small-scale puppet shows. You can follow their process – and even give it a go yourself!

Activities

Here we have a host of stay-at-home activity guides to help keep you and your children entertained.
Our guides are easy to follow and use simple materials (most of which can be found around the home) and are aimed at (ages 3-6) with focussed adult help and KS2 children (ages 7-11) to use independently. The guides can be filtered using the filters below.
Please share your completed makes with us and, with your permission, we will upload them to our gallery. Email your photos to info@littleangeltheatre.com or share them with us on Twitter, Facebook or Instagram.
